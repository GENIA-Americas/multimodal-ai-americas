# Glossary

## A

**AI Corridor of the Americas**
A cross-border network of policymakers, industry leaders, and capital aligned around industrial AI leadership originating within the Western Hemisphere.

**AmerinLI**
GENIA Americas' multilingual NLP dataset covering indigenous and regional languages across the Americas.

**AmeriXLM**
Multilingual language model trained on Latin American Spanish and Brazilian Portuguese regional corpora.

## D

**Digital Non-Alignment**
The organizing principle of GENIA Americas: reduce structural dependency on external AI infrastructure while enabling full global collaboration.

## F

**Federated Learning**
A machine learning approach where models are trained across decentralized data sources without raw data ever leaving its jurisdiction.

## G

**GENIA Americas Corporation**
Founded 2018. The connective infrastructure of artificial intelligence across the Western Hemisphere.

**Glapagos**
AI DevOps and data orchestration platform built specifically for the Americas. Handles multi-jurisdiction deployment, federated ingestion, and compliance enforcement.

## H

**H.Res.649**
U.S. House Resolution 649 (118th Congress) — analyzed in policy/hres649.md.

## M

**Municipal Data Lab**
A framework for city-level AI experimentation and deployment, designed for Latin American municipal governments.

## R

**RaceFor.AI**
The hemispheric policy and industry network. 35 nations, 12+ regions. Coordinates AI strategy across the Western Hemisphere.

**Regional AI Strategy**
The first comprehensive framework for responsible, representative, inclusive, and scalable AI development across the Western Hemisphere.
