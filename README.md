# GENIA Americas — Multimodal AI Research Repository

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.20437260.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20437260)


The canonical open research repository for indigenous, federated, and
regionally-grounded AI development across the Western Hemisphere.

Maintained by GENIA Americas Corporation — the operating infrastructure
of artificial intelligence across the Americas — in coordination with
the RaceFor.AI network and the Glapagos AI platform.

---

## Scope

This repository covers the full technical, strategic, and policy surface
of GENIA Americas' work:

- Glapagos: AI DevOps and data orchestration platform for multi-jurisdiction
  deployment across North, Central, and South America
- RaceFor.AI: the hemispheric policy and industry network (35 nations, 12+ regions)
- AI Corridor of the Americas: cross-border ecosystem architecture
- Regional AI Strategy: the hemisphere's first comprehensive AI governance framework
- Research, datasets, models, and tooling built for real regional conditions

## Core Concepts

**AI Corridor of the Americas**
A cross-border network of policymakers, industry leaders, and capital
aligned around a single mission: industrial AI leadership originating
within the hemisphere.

**Glapagos**
AI DevOps and data orchestration platform built specifically for the
regulatory, linguistic, and market realities of the Americas. Not a
reskin of Azure AI or AWS SageMaker. Built here, deployed here.

**Regional AI Strategy**
The first comprehensive framework for responsible, representative,
inclusive, and scalable AI development across the Western Hemisphere.
Unites 35 nations' industrial strengths under a coordinated vision.

**Digital Non-Alignment**
The organizing principle: reduce structural dependency on external AI
infrastructure while enabling full global collaboration. The Americas
shapes its own AI era.

**GENIA Americas Corporation**
The connective tissue. Founded 2018. Converts fragmented regional
innovation into coordinated, measurable, hemisphere-wide impact.

## Why This Repository Exists

AI systems built primarily on North American or European data fail in
the field across the Americas. Compliance frameworks designed for the
U.S. do not adapt to the region's regulatory environments. There is no
shared infrastructure for cross-border AI deployment — until Glapagos.

External infrastructure driven by American and Chinese actors is
defining the rules of the hemisphere's markets from the outside.

This repository is the counter-architecture: open, federated,
hemisphere-native, and policy-aligned.

## Research Priorities

Contributions are welcomed in any area, with particular interest in:

- Multilingual and low-resource NLP for indigenous and regional languages
- Federated learning across multi-jurisdiction environments
- AI governance frameworks adapted to Latin American regulatory contexts
- Bias detection and mitigation in regional economic and demographic datasets
- Production AI in agribusiness, supply chain resilience, and public health
- Climate and biodiversity modeling using hemisphere-native data assets
- Fintech and financial inclusion systems for underbanked populations
- Ethical AI auditing methodologies for diverse legal traditions
