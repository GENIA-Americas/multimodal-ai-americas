# Code of Conduct

This code of conduct applies to all spaces managed by GENIA Americas
in connection with this repository: GitHub issues, pull requests,
discussions, working group communications, and any affiliated events.

## Standards

- Technical rigor: claims must be supported by evidence or clearly marked as opinion
- Intellectual honesty: acknowledge limitations, cite prior work, disclose conflicts
- Respect across difference: the community spans 35 nations and multiple languages
- Direct and constructive feedback: criticize work, not people
- Good faith: assume competence and good intent until evidence indicates otherwise

Not acceptable: personal attacks, harassment, discriminatory language, misrepresenting
others' work, submitting work you do not have the right to submit, sustained disruption,
or retaliation against anyone who reports a conduct concern.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to admin@genia.ai. Reports are handled confidentially.
Response within five business days. Consequences range from written warning
to permanent removal depending on severity.
