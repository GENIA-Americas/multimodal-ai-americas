# Glapagos Platform Overview

AI DevOps and data orchestration platform built for the Americas.
Developed by GENIA Americas Corporation.

https://www.glapagos.com

---

## What Glapagos Is

Glapagos is the technology backbone of the AI Corridor of the Americas.
It is an AI DevOps and data orchestration platform designed specifically
for organizations operating across North, Central, and South America.

It is not a reskin of Azure AI, AWS SageMaker, or Google Vertex. Those
platforms were built for U.S. and European regulatory environments, with
training data that underrepresents the Americas. Glapagos was built
here, for here.

Currently deployed across 12+ regions in the hemisphere. Organizations
use it to automate workflows, deploy machine learning in production, and
build industry-specific AI without rebuilding their data infrastructure.

## Core Capabilities

### Multi-Jurisdiction Deployment

Glapagos handles the regulatory complexity of operating across multiple
legal environments simultaneously. Compliance frameworks are built into
the deployment pipeline, not bolted on afterward.

Supported regulatory contexts include data protection laws, sector-specific
regulations, and government AI procurement standards across participating
nations.

### Regional Data Integration

The platform ingests and normalizes data from regional industrial sources:
agriculture, energy, mining, logistics, fintech, and public services.
Data pipelines are configured for the actual data formats, standards, and
quality levels present in each market — not idealized conditions.

### Machine Learning Lifecycle Management

End-to-end MLOps:

- Model training on regional datasets
- Experiment tracking and version control
- Evaluation against regional performance benchmarks
- Production deployment with monitoring
- Drift detection and retraining triggers

### Cross-Border Collaboration Infrastructure

Federated architecture that allows organizations across jurisdictions to
collaborate on AI development without centralizing sensitive data. Each
node retains data sovereignty. The network gains collective intelligence.

### Open Integration

Open-source core. Connects to existing business systems, ERPs, CRMs,
government databases, and sector-specific platforms. No forced migration
to a new stack.

## Architecture Principles

**Federated by design**
Data does not have to leave its jurisdiction to contribute to shared
models. Federated learning and privacy-preserving computation are
first-class features, not optional add-ons.

**Modular and composable**
Organizations onboard the capabilities they need. The platform scales
from a single-use-case pilot to enterprise-wide deployment without
architectural rework.

**Regional compliance embedded**
Compliance is a property of the deployment configuration, not a
post-hoc check. Jurisdiction-specific rules are encoded in the pipeline.

**Open-source core**
The platform is built on an open-source foundation. Vendor lock-in is
a structural risk the hemisphere cannot afford. Contributions to the
core are welcomed.

## Deployment Model

Glapagos starts with a pilot: identify one costly operational bottleneck,
prove AI impact in days, then scale.

1. **Discovery** — Infrastructure assessment, high-impact entry point
   identification, compliance environment mapping
2. **Pilot** — Single-use-case deployment. Measurable outcome defined
   upfront. Timeline: days to weeks.
3. **Evaluation** — Outcome review against defined metrics. Go/no-go
   for expansion.
4. **Scale** — Modular expansion across use cases, departments, and
   jurisdictions.
