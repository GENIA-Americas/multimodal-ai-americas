# Partners

AI Corridor of the Americas
GENIA Americas Corporation / RaceFor.AI

## Partnership Types

Network Member: Full organizational membership. Holds Network Council seat.
Research Partner: Academic or research institution contributing to the repository.
Technology Partner: Technology organizations whose platforms integrate with Glapagos.
Policy Partner: Government agencies and multilateral bodies aligned with Corridor policy.
Implementation Partner: Organizations that implement Corridor frameworks on behalf of clients.

## Becoming a Partner

1. Contact admin@genia.ai with organization description, partnership type, and intended contribution
2. Introductory call with GENIA Americas
3. Formal proposal submitted to Network Council
4. Council reviews at next quarterly meeting

All partners must accept the Ethics Charter. Network Members must also
designate a Network Council representative and commit to working group participation.

Contact: admin@genia.ai
