# Working Groups

AI Corridor of the Americas
GENIA Americas Corporation / RaceFor.AI

Working groups develop technical standards, coordinate research, and
run sector-specific programs. Open to any researcher, engineer, or
practitioner with relevant expertise. Membership does not require
organizational affiliation with GENIA Americas.

To join: open a Working Group Request issue or contact admin@genia.ai.

## Active Working Groups

### NLP and Language
Scope: Multilingual AI, benchmarks, low-resource and indigenous language research.
Priorities: AmeriNLI maintenance, IndigenousLM development, regional corpus construction.
Chair: Open — Bi-weekly Tuesdays 15:00 BRT
Activity: research/benchmarks/nlp/, models/regional-nlp/
