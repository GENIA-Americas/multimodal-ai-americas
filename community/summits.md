# Race For AI Summits

GENIA Americas Corporation / RaceFor.AI

## Overview

The Race For AI Summit is the annual convening of the AI Corridor of
the Americas. Policymakers, researchers, engineers, investors, and civil
society align on strategy, share implementation outcomes, and advance
governance standards. The standard for a presentation is documented
outcomes, not projected impacts.

## Summit Record

### Race For AI Summit 2024

Theme: From Framework to Deployment
Format: Regional hybrid

Key outcomes:
- Launch of the Regional AI Strategy v1.0
- Adoption of the Ethics Charter by founding Network Members
- Municipal Lab pilots announced in three cities
- Working group structure formalized
- Research repository established

Sessions:
- Opening: The State of AI in the Americas — Felipe Castro Quiles, GENIA Americas
- Policy: H.Res.649 and the Hemispheric AI Governance Agenda
- Technical: Glapagos Platform Architecture and Roadmap
- Research: AmeriNLI and RegioNER — Regional Benchmarks for NLP
- Case Study: Municipal Service AI Pilots — Early Lessons
- Governance: Network Council Structure and Working Group Formation

Materials available on request: admin@genia.ai
